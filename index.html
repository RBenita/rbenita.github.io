Roi Benita

PhD Student @ Technion – Israel Institute of Technology

I am a PhD student at the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology.
My research lies at the intersection of generative models and signal processing. I am particularly interested in diffusion models, inverse problems, and time-series modeling, aiming to improve the understanding and efficiency of generative methods.
